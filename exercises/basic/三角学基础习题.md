## 1. 三角比与三角函数 | Trigonometric Ratios and Functions

### 典型题目1 | Example 1

**题目：已知直角三角形中，$\sin\theta = \frac{3}{5}$，求 $\cos\theta$ 和 $\tan\theta$。**

**Problem: In a right triangle, given $\sin\theta = \frac{3}{5}$, find $\cos\theta$ and $\tan\theta$.**

**解答 | Solution:**

- $\sin\theta = \frac{3}{5}$，设对边为 $3$，斜边为 $5$，邻边为 $4$。
- $\cos\theta = \frac{4}{5}$，$\tan\theta = \frac{3}{4}$。

---

### 典型题目2 | Example 2

**题目：化简 $\sin^2x + \cos^2x$。**

**Problem: Simplify $\sin^2x + \cos^2x$.**

**解答 | Solution:**

- $\sin^2x + \cos^2x = 1$

---

### 典型题目3 | Example 3

**题目：解三角方程 $2\sin x = 1$，$x \in [0, 2\pi)$。**

**Problem: Solve the trigonometric equation $2\sin x = 1$ for $x \in [0, 2\pi)$.**

**解答 | Solution:**

- $\sin x = \frac{1}{2}$，$x = \frac{\pi}{6}, \frac{5\pi}{6}$。

---

### 典型题目4 | Example 4

**题目：已知三角形两边 $a=7, b=10$，夹角 $C=60^\circ$，求第三边 $c$。**

**Problem: In triangle $ABC$, given $a=7$, $b=10$, and included angle $C=60^\circ$, find the third side $c$.**

**解答 | Solution:**

- 余弦定理：$c^2 = a^2 + b^2 - 2ab\cos C$
- $c^2 = 49 + 100 - 2 \times 7 \times 10 \times \frac{1}{2} = 149 - 70 = 79$
- $c = \sqrt{79}$

---

### 典型题目5 | Example 5

**题目：画出 $y = \sin x$ 在 $[0, 2\pi]$ 上的图像，并说明其主要特征。**

**Problem: Draw the graph of $y = \sin x$ on $[0, 2\pi]$ and describe its main features.**

**解答 | Solution:**

- 图像为周期性波动，最大值 $1$，最小值 $-1$，周期 $2\pi$，在 $x=0$ 处为 $0$。
- The graph is a periodic wave, maximum $1$, minimum $-1$, period $2\pi$, passes through $0$ at $x=0$.

### 2.1.4 典型例题 | Example Problems

**例1 | Example 1**:

- **题目**：利用 $\sin^2x + \cos^2x = 1$，化简 $1 - \sin^2x$。
- **Problem**: Use $\sin^2x + \cos^2x = 1$ to simplify $1 - \sin^2x$.

**解答 | Solution**:

- $1 - \sin^2x = \cos^2x$

---

**例2 | Example 2**:

- **题目**：证明 $1 + \tan^2x = \sec^2x$。
- **Problem**: Prove $1 + \tan^2x = \sec^2x$.

**解答 | Solution**:

- $\tan x = \frac{\sin x}{\cos x}$，$\sec x = \frac{1}{\cos x}$
- $1 + \tan^2x = 1 + \frac{\sin^2x}{\cos^2x} = \frac{\cos^2x + \sin^2x}{\cos^2x} = \frac{1}{\cos^2x} = \sec^2x$

## 1.1 直角三角形中的三角比 | Trigonometric Ratios in Right Triangles

### 权威定义 | Authoritative Definition

- **中文**：在直角三角形中，锐角的三角比定义为：
  - 正弦（sin）：对边比斜边
  - 余弦（cos）：邻边比斜边
  - 正切（tan）：对边比邻边
  
- **English**: In a right triangle, the trigonometric ratios of an acute angle are defined as:
  - Sine (sin): opposite side / hypotenuse
  - Cosine (cos): adjacent side / hypotenuse
  - Tangent (tan): opposite side / adjacent side

- **权威来源 | Reference**：
  - [Wikipedia: Trigonometric functions](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Trigonometric_functions)
  - [MathWorld: Trigonometric Functions](https://mathworld.wolfram.com/TrigonometricFunctions.html)

---

### 国际对比 | International Comparison

- **中国教材**：
  - “锐角的三角比：在直角三角形中，锐角的正弦、余弦、正切分别定义为对边比斜边、邻边比斜边、对边比邻边。”
- **US Common Core**：
  - “Define the sine, cosine, and tangent of an acute angle in a right triangle as the ratio of the length of the side opposite/adjacent to the angle to the length of the hypotenuse.”
- **A-level (UK)**：
  - “The trigonometric ratios in a right-angled triangle: sin θ = opposite/hypotenuse, cos θ = adjacent/hypotenuse, tan θ = opposite/adjacent.”

---

### 标准解释 | Standard Explanation

- 三角比是连接角度与边长关系的基本工具，是三角函数的基础。
- Trigonometric ratios are fundamental tools connecting angles and side lengths, forming the basis of trigonometric functions.

---

### 典型例题 | Example Problems

-**例1 | Example 1**

- **题目**：在直角三角形 $ABC$ 中，$\angle C = 90^\circ$，$AB = 13$，$AC = 5$，$BC = 12$，求 $\sin A, \cos A, \tan A$。
- **Problem**: In right triangle $ABC$, $\angle C = 90^\circ$, $AB = 13$, $AC = 5$, $BC = 12$. Find $\sin A, \cos A, \tan A$.

**解答 | Solution**:

- $\sin A = \frac{BC}{AB} = \frac{12}{13}$
- $\cos A = \frac{AC}{AB} = \frac{5}{13}$
- $\tan A = \frac{BC}{AC} = \frac{12}{5}$

## 1.2 任意角的三角函数 | Trigonometric Functions of Any Angle

### 1.2.1 权威定义 | Authoritative Definition

- **中文**：任意角的三角函数定义为单位圆上与角对应的点的坐标：
  - $\theta$ 角的终边与单位圆交于点 $P(x, y)$，则
    - $\sin\theta = y$
    - $\cos\theta = x$
    - $\tan\theta = \frac{y}{x},\ x \neq 0$
- **English**: The trigonometric functions of any angle are defined using the unit circle:
  - For an angle $\theta$, let $P(x, y)$ be the intersection of its terminal side and the unit circle, then
    - $\sin\theta = y$
    - $\cos\theta = x$
    - $\tan\theta = \frac{y}{x},\ x \neq 0$

- **权威来源 | Reference**：
  - [Wikipedia: Trigonometric functions](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Trigonometric_functions)
  - [MathWorld: Trigonometric Functions](https://mathworld.wolfram.com/TrigonometricFunctions.html)

---

### 1.2.2 国际对比 | International Comparison

- **中国教材**：
  - “任意角的三角函数：以单位圆定义 $\theta$ 的三角函数，$\theta$ 的终边与单位圆交于 $P(x, y)$，$\sin\theta = y, \cos\theta = x, \tan\theta = \frac{y}{x}$。”
- **US Common Core**：
  - “Extend definitions of sine and cosine to all real numbers using the unit circle.”
- **A-level (UK)**：
  - “Define trigonometric functions for all angles using the unit circle.”

---

### 1.2.3 标准解释 | Standard Explanation

- 单位圆定义将三角函数从直角三角形推广到任意角，便于处理负角、超过 $90^\circ$ 的角和周期性。
- The unit circle definition extends trigonometric functions to all angles, allowing for negative angles, angles greater than $90^\circ$, and periodicity.

---

### 1.2.4 典型例题 | Example Problems

**例1 | Example 1**:

- **题目**：已知 $\theta = 150^\circ$，求 $\sin\theta, \cos\theta, \tan\theta$。
- **Problem**: Given $\theta = 150^\circ$, find $\sin\theta, \cos\theta, \tan\theta$.

**解答 | Solution**:

- $\sin 150^\circ = \sin(180^\circ - 30^\circ) = \sin 30^\circ = \frac{1}{2}$
- $\cos 150^\circ = -\cos 30^\circ = -\frac{\sqrt{3}}{2}$
- $\tan 150^\circ = -\tan 30^\circ = -\frac{1}{\sqrt{3}}$

---

**例2 | Example 2**:

- **题目**：画出 $y = \sin\theta$ 在 $\theta \in [-2\pi, 2\pi]$ 上的图像，并说明其周期性。
- **Problem**: Draw the graph of $y = \sin\theta$ for $\theta \in [-2\pi, 2\pi]$ and describe its periodicity.

**解答 | Solution**:

- $y = \sin\theta$ 的周期为 $2\pi$，图像在每 $2\pi$ 重复一次。
- The period of $y = \sin\theta$ is $2\pi$, the graph repeats every $2\pi$.

## 1.3 三角函数的定义与性质 | Definition and Properties of Trigonometric Functions

### 1.3.1 权威定义 | Authoritative Definition

- **中文**：三角函数是以角度（或弧度）为自变量的周期函数，包括正弦、余弦、正切等。其基本性质包括周期性、奇偶性、有界性等。
- **English**: Trigonometric functions are periodic functions with angle (or radian) as the independent variable, including sine, cosine, tangent, etc. Their basic properties include periodicity, parity, boundedness, etc.

- **权威来源 | Reference**：
  - [Wikipedia: Trigonometric functions](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Trigonometric_functions)
  - [MathWorld: Trigonometric Functions](https://mathworld.wolfram.com/TrigonometricFunctions.html)

---

### 1.3.2 国际对比 | International Comparison

- **中国教材**：
  - “三角函数的定义域为全体实数，正弦、余弦的周期为 $2\pi$，正切的周期为 $\pi$，正弦为奇函数，余弦为偶函数。”
- **US Common Core**：
  - “Understand the periodicity and symmetry of sine and cosine functions, and their domains and ranges.”
- **A-level (UK)**：
  - “Sine and cosine are periodic with period $2\pi$, tangent with period $\pi$; define and use even/odd properties.”

---

### 1.3.3 标准解释 | Standard Explanation

- 三角函数具有周期性（$forall x,\ \sin(x+2\pi)=\sin x$），有界性（$|\sin x|\leq 1$），奇偶性（$tan(-x)=-\tan x$，$tan x$ 为奇函数）。
- Trigonometric functions are periodic ($\sin(x+2\pi)=\sin x$), bounded ($|\sin x|\leq 1$), and have parity ($\tan(-x)=-\tan x$, tangent is odd).

---

### 1.3.4 典型例题 | Example Problems

**例1 | Example 1**:

- **题目**：写出 $\sin x, \cos x, \tan x$ 的周期、定义域、值域。
- **Problem**: State the period, domain, and range of $\sin x, \cos x, \tan x$.

**解答 | Solution**:

- $\sin x, \cos x$：周期 $2\pi$，定义域 $\mathbb{R}$，值域 $[-1,1]$
- $\tan x$：周期 $\pi$，定义域 $x\neq k\pi+\frac{\pi}{2}$，值域 $\mathbb{R}$

---

**例2 | Example 2**:

- **题目**：判断 $\sin(-x)$、$\cos(-x)$、$\tan(-x)$ 的奇偶性。
- **Problem**: Determine the parity (even/odd) of $\sin(-x)$, $\cos(-x)$, $\tan(-x)$.

**解答 | Solution**:

- $\sin(-x) = -\sin x$（奇函数），$\cos(-x) = \cos x$（偶函数），$\tan(-x) = -\tan x$（奇函数）
- $\sin(-x)$ is odd, $\cos(-x)$ is even, $\tan(-x)$ is odd.
